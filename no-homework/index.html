A2 — Elementar

Kundalik muloqot uchun zarur grammatika

01

Present Simple — Qoidalar va amaliyot

Grammatika▶
Tuzilish
✅ I/You/We/They + V1 He/She/It + V+s/es ❌ I/You/We/They + don't + V1 He/She/It + doesn't + V1 ❓ Do + I/you/we/they + V1? Does + he/she/it + V1? He/she/it qo'shimchalari: → odatda: +s (works, reads, eats) → -ch,-sh,-s,-x,-o: +es (watches, washes, goes) → konsonant+y → ies: study→studies, fly→flies
Kalit vaqt so'zlari (adverbs of frequency)
always (doimo) → usually (odatda) → often (tez-tez) → sometimes (ba'zan) → rarely (kamdan-kam) → never (hech qachon) I always have breakfast. She never smokes. How often do you exercise?
  • I drink coffee every morning. — Men har kuni ertalab qahva ichaman.
  • She doesn't watch TV much. — U televisor ko'rmaydi deyarli.
  • Does he play football? — U futbol o'ynaydimi?
  • The shop opens at 9. — Do'kon 9 da ochiladi.
  • Water boils at 100°C. — Suv 100°C da qaynaydi.
02

Past Simple — O'tgan zamon

Grammatika▶
Muntazam fe'llar (Regular)
V1 + ed (yoki d, ied, pped…) work→worked play→played live→lived study→studied stop→stopped travel→travelled Qoidalar: -e bilan tugasa +d, konsonant+y→ied, qisqa+konsonant→ikkilantir
Nomuntazam fe'llar (Irregular) — 40 ta asosiy
be→was/were | go→went | come→came | see→saw eat→ate | have→had | do→did | get→got give→gave | make→made | take→took | know→knew say→said | find→found| think→thought buy→bought | bring→brought | feel→felt leave→left | lose→lost | meet→met | put→put read→read | run→ran | sit→sat | stand→stood tell→told | write→wrote| drive→drove| grow→grew
  • I visited Paris last summer. — O'tgan yoz Parijga bordim.
  • She didn't come to school. — U maktabga kelmadi.
  • Did you enjoy the film? — Filmdan zavqlandingizmi?
  • We met them at the airport. — Biz ularni aeroportda kutib oldik.
03

Artikl — a / an / the / —

Grammatika▶
Qachon qaysi artikl?
a/an → noaniq, birinchi marta aytilgan, bir dona the → aniq, avval aytilgan, yagona (sun, moon, President) — → ko'plik umumiy, nomuvofiq nom (music, love, Tashkent) a + undosh tovush: a book, a car, a university (/juː/) an + unli tovush: an apple, an hour (/aʊər/), an MBA
  • I saw a dog. The dog was big. — Bir it ko'rdim. O'sha it katta edi.
  • The sun rises in the east. — Quyosh sharqdan chiqadi.
  • She plays the piano. / She plays football.
  • I love music. (— music = umumiy)
  • He's an honest man. — U halol odam. (an + unli)

  • I'll meet you at the café at 3.

B1 — O'rta daraja

Murakkab zamon shakllari va modal fe'llar

01

Present Perfect vs Past Simple

Grammatika▶
Present Perfect: have/has + V3
Kalit so'zlar: ever, never, already, yet, just, since, for, recently, so far, lately, in my life I have just arrived. (hozir gina) Have you ever been to Japan? (hayot tajribasi) She hasn't finished yet. (hali tugamagan) We've lived here for 5 years / since 2019.
Farqi
Past Simple → aniq vaqt belgilangan (yesterday, last year, in 2020) Pres. Perfect → vaqt noaniq YOKI natija muhim ❌ I have seen him yesterday. ✅ I saw him yesterday. ✅ I have seen that film. (qachonligini bilmaymiz, natija muhim)
  • I've lost my keys! — Kalitlarimni yo'qotib qo'ydim! (hozirgacha yo'q)
  • I lost my keys yesterday. — Kecha kalitlarimni yo'qotdim.
  • Have you ever eaten durian?
  • She's been a teacher for 10 years.
02

Past Continuous & Past Perfect

Grammatika▶
Past Continuous: was/were + V-ing
→ O'tmishda muayyan vaqtda davom etgan harakat I was sleeping at 10 pm. They were watching TV when I called. While she was cooking, I was reading.
Past Perfect: had + V3
→ O'tmishda boshqa harakatdan oldin sodir bo'lgan When I arrived, the film had already started. She had never seen snow before she came to Russia. By the time he called, I had left.
03

Modal Fe'llar — To'liq qo'llanma

Grammatika▶
Hozir / Kelajak uchun
can → qodir (I can swim.) could → qodir edi / muloyim iltimos (Could you help?) may → ehtimol yoki ruxsat (It may rain. May I leave?) might → kuchsiz ehtimol (I might come.) must → majburiy (You must wear a seatbelt.) mustn't → taqiq (You mustn't smoke here.) should → tavsiya (You should see a doctor.) ought to→ should ga yaqin (You ought to apologise.) will → kelajak / taklif (I'll help you.) would → shartli / so'rov (Would you like tea?)
Ehtimol darajasi
100% → will/must be ("He must be tired.") 80% → should be 50% → may be 30% → might be / could be 0% → can't be ("She can't be serious!")
04

Passive Voice — Barcha zamonlar

Grammatika▶
be + V3
Pres. Simple: am/is/are + V3 → English is spoken worldwide. Past Simple: was/were + V3 → The letter was sent. Pres. Perf.: have/has been+V3 → It has been solved. Past Perfect: had been + V3 → The work had been done. Future: will be + V3 → It will be announced. Modal: modal+be+V3 → It should be checked.
  • Active: They make iPhones in China. → Passive: iPhones are made in China.
  • Active: Someone stole my wallet. → Passive: My wallet was stolen.
  • The bridge is being built. — Ko'prik qurilmoqda.

B2 — Yuqori O'rta

Murakkab grammatika va nutq mahorati

01

Conditionals — 4 tur shartli gap

Grammatika▶
To'liq jadval
0 — Umumiy haqiqat: If + Pres.Simple, Pres.Simple "If you freeze water, it becomes ice." 1 — Haqiqiy shart (kelajak): If + Pres.Simple, will/can/may + V "If it rains, I'll stay home." 2 — Xayoliy hozir (afsos yoki tush): If + Past Simple, would/could/might + V "If I had wings, I would fly to you." "If I were you, I wouldn't do that." (were — subjunctive) 3 — O'tmishda bo'lmagan (afsus): If + Past Perfect, would/could have + V3 "If I had studied, I would have passed." Mixed: If + Past Perfect, would + V (o'tmish → hozir) "If she had left earlier, she would be here now."
02

Reported Speech — Bilvosita nutq

Grammatika▶
Zamon siljishi (backshift)
"I am tired." → He said he was tired. "I work here." → She said she worked there. "I am working." → He said he was working. "I will help." → She said she would help. "I can swim." → He said he could swim. "I have finished." → She said she had finished. "I ate lunch." → He said he had eaten lunch.
So'roq gaplar
"Are you coming?" → He asked if/whether I was coming. "Where do you live?" → She asked where I lived. "What did you do?" → He asked what I had done.

C1 — Ilg'or daraja

01

Inversion — Inversiya

Grammatika▶
Salbiy so'z boshda tursa
Never have I seen such beauty. (I have never seen…) Rarely does he make mistakes. (He rarely makes…) Not only did she win, but she also broke a record. Hardly had I arrived when it started raining. No sooner had he left than she called. Only then did I understand the truth. Under no circumstances should you do this.
Inversiya — rasmiy yozma uslub va adabiy nutqda keng qo'llaniladi.
02

Cleft Sentences — Ajratib ta'kidlash

Grammatika▶
It is/was … that/who
Normal: She broke the vase. Cleft: It was SHE who broke the vase. It was THE VASE that she broke. It was IN THE KITCHEN that she broke it. What-cleft: What I need is some rest. What surprised me was his reaction.
